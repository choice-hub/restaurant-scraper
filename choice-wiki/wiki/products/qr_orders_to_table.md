# Product: QR Orders to Table

## Overview
Customers scan a unique QR code at their table and order directly from their smartphone. Orders arrive instantly at the kitchen or POS system — no waiter needed for order-taking.

## Key Features

- **Per-table QR codes** — Each table has a unique code; order is tied to table number
- **No app download** — Works in any mobile browser
- **Smart order display** — Shows table number, new vs. returning customer flag
- **Accept/reject orders** — Staff can manage order flow directly in the app
- **POS integration** — Orders go straight to kitchen display or POS
- **Online payment** — Customers pay at checkout, or pay by cash at the end
- **Tipping** — Digital tip prompts with configurable percentages
- **Upsell engine** — Recommendations and wishlist increase average check
- **Customer data** — Each order builds the customer database automatically

## How It Works
1. Customer sits down and scans the table QR code
2. Browses the digital menu, adds items to cart
3. Places order → appears on staff tablet/POS
4. Staff accepts order → kitchen prepares
5. Waiter serves the food
