# Product: QR Orders to Table

**URL:** https://choiceqr.com/products-en/qr-orders-to-table/

## Overview
Customers scan a QR code at their table and order directly from their smartphone. Reduce operational costs by over 30% and increase profitability. Also works poolside, on terraces, or anywhere — no waiter needed for order-taking.

## Key Features

- **Per-table QR codes** — Each table has a unique code; order is tied to table number
- **No app download** — Works in any mobile browser
- **30% lower personnel costs** — Customers order themselves; staff just brings the food
- **POS integration** — Orders go straight to kitchen display or POS; no manual entry
- **Accept/reject orders** — Staff manage order flow directly in the app
- **Online payment** — Customers can pay at checkout or by cash at the end
- **Tipping** — Digital tip prompts with configurable percentages
- **Upsell engine** — Dish recommendations and wishlist increase average check
- **Customer data** — Every order automatically builds the CRM database
- **Speeds up table turnover** — Ideal for terraces, food courts, busy periods when staff are stretched

## How It Works
1. Customer scans the QR code at their table
2. Browses the digital menu, adds items to cart
3. Places order → appears instantly on staff tablet/POS
4. Staff accepts → kitchen prepares
5. Waiter delivers the food
