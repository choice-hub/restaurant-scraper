# Product: QR Payments at the Table

**URL:** https://choiceqr.com/products-en/qr-payments-at-the-table/

## Overview
Boost table turnover by 30%, streamline bill splitting, and increase tips by 20% by offering QR payments straight from the table. Guests pay whenever it's convenient for them — saving 20–30% of their time.

## Key Features

- **Instant bill viewing** — Scan QR → see full itemized bill in 10 seconds, no waiting for the check
- **No app, no registration** — Works in any mobile browser, with all banks, including Apple Pay and Google Pay
- **Bill splitting** — Guests split equally or assign specific items per person
- **Per-person tipping** — Each diner tips independently; staff and restaurant can both receive tips
- **Increased tips** — Guests leave more tips through online methods even without spare change (up to +20%)
- **Real-time notifications** — Staff alerted instantly when a table pays
- **POS integration** — Payment syncs with Poster, Syrve, Storyous, Dotykačka, R-Keeper, etc.
- **Customer data capture** — Builds CRM database automatically
- **Zero commission** from Choice — only standard bank acquiring fees apply

## How It Works
1. Waiter delivers bill or customer requests it
2. Customer scans table QR code
3. Views itemized bill, selects what to pay, adds optional tip
4. Pays with card / Apple Pay / Google Pay
5. Staff receives instant payment notification; table marked as paid
