# Product: QR Payment at the Table

## Overview
Guests pay their bill by scanning a QR code at the table — no waiting for the check, no card terminal pass-around. Works with bill splitting and integrated tipping.

## Key Features

- **Instant bill viewing** — Scan QR → see full itemized bill
- **No app, no registration** — Works in any mobile browser
- **Bill splitting** — Guests can split equally or assign specific items to each person
- **Per-person tipping** — Each diner tips independently
- **Waiter tips** — Staff tip option at checkout (goes directly to team)
- **Restaurant tips** — Separate house tip option (for shared bonuses)
- **Multiple payment methods** — Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Real-time notifications** — Staff alerted when table pays
- **POS integration** — Payment syncs with Poster, Syrve, Servio, Profit, etc.
- **Customer data capture** — Builds CRM database automatically
- **Zero commission** from Choice — only standard bank acquiring fees apply

## How It Works
1. Waiter delivers bill (or customer requests it)
2. Customer scans table QR code
3. Views itemized bill, selects items to pay for
4. Adds tip (optional)
5. Pays with card/Apple Pay/Google Pay
6. Staff receives payment confirmation; table marked as paid
