## TL;DR
Online reservation system with calendar, automatic confirmation, deposits against no-shows, and a single guest database. **Available from STANDARD** (or as Reservation module). Main pain: phone reservations = lost guests and chaos. Main argument: more reservations without picking up the phone + own guest database.

## What It Is
Online reservation system with a clear calendar, automatic booking confirmation, capacity management, optional deposits, and integration with other Choice tools (web, QR, delivery) — all synced into one customer database.

## Who It's For
- Taking reservations by phone, Instagram, or paper book
- Suffering from no-shows or holding tables unnecessarily
- Want an overview of occupancy, possibly across multiple branches
- Paying for an expensive solution just for a basic calendar

## Key Features
**Booking Channels**
- Own website embedded widget
- Direct shareable link (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
- QR code (printed at entrance or on tables)
- Google Business Profile "Reserve a Table" button

**Management**
- Interactive calendar with table map / floor plan
- Day-by-day management with shift and time-slot control
- Automatic confirmation sent to guest; reminders reduce no-shows
- Post-visit review request sent automatically
- Deposits or card guarantee to prevent no-shows

**Guest Database**
- Single database built automatically across all channels (QR, delivery, reservations)
- Works across multiple branches with central overview

**No Own Website Needed**
- Booking link works independently — restaurants without a website can still accept online reservations

## Use Cases
- Bistros and restaurants with fast table turnover
- Fine dining with reservations weeks ahead
- Event bars (table reservation for the evening)
- Multiple branches — central overview

## Real Example
A restaurant takes reservations only by phone — during lunch nobody answers and a few guests go elsewhere each day. They install Choice reservations: guests book online even from their couch in the evening, the system sends reminders, evening tables have a deposit set. Result: more reservations, fewer no-shows, and staff focus on guests instead of the phone. Real cases show reservation increases of hundreds of percent.

## Sales Arguments
**Strong:**
- More reservations without picking up the phone
- Fewer no-shows thanks to deposits and reminders
- All in one system — web, reservations, orders, QR
- Simple setup, Czech/Slovak support 7 days a week

**Common objections:**
- *"Phone and paper is enough."* → Guests (especially younger) don't like calling and want to book anytime — even from the couch in the evening. You also have a clear overview without chaos.
- *"We have few reservations, not worth it."* → Often that's precisely because there's no system. We have real cases where reservations grew by hundreds of percent.
