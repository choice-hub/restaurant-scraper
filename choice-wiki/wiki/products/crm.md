# Product: CRM System for Restaurants

## Overview
Choice's built-in CRM automatically collects customer data from every order, table visit, and interaction — building an owned customer database without any manual work.

## Key Features

**Customer Database**
- Name, phone, email captured automatically on order
- Full order history and spending patterns per customer
- Favorite dishes and preferences tracked
- Staff notes on seating preferences, allergies, habits
- Review history linked to customer profile

**Analytics & Insights**
- Menu popularity analysis from real order data
- Channel comparison: dine-in vs. delivery vs. takeaway
- Marketing campaign effectiveness tracking
- New vs. returning customer breakdown

**Data Management**
- Export to XLSX / CSV at any time
- Import existing customer data
- Customer base segmentation (coming soon) — filter by: new guests, last order date, order frequency, spend level

**Marketing Integration**
- Direct email campaigns from the platform
- Push notification targeting
- Loyalty program integration
- Promo code distribution to segments

## How It Works
Every order through Choice automatically adds or updates the customer's profile. Staff can also add manual notes. The dashboard gives a full view of each guest's history.

## Business Impact
| Metric | Improvement |
|--------|------------|
| Repeat order increase | +60% |
| Cost vs. acquiring new customers | 6x cheaper to retain |
| Revenue growth via newsletters/loyalty | +35% |

## Plans
CRM database available on all plans. Advanced segmentation and targeted campaigns available on **Smart and Pro plans**.
