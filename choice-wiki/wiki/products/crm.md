## TL;DR
Choice automatically builds the restaurant's own customer database from every order and interaction — no manual work. **Main pain:** all customer data belongs to Wolt/Bolt, not the restaurant. **Main argument:** you own your customers and can reach them directly.

## What It Is
Every order through Choice (QR, delivery, takeaway, reservations) automatically adds or updates the customer's profile. The restaurant gets a full, owned CRM — not just order data that lives on someone else's platform.

## Key Features

**Customer Database**
- Name, phone, email captured automatically on every order
- Full order history and spending patterns per customer
- Favourite dishes and preferences tracked
- Staff notes (seating preferences, allergies, habits)
- Review history linked to customer profile
- Export to XLSX / CSV at any time

**Analytics & Insights**
- Menu popularity from real order data
- Channel comparison: dine-in vs. delivery vs. takeaway
- Marketing campaign effectiveness
- New vs. returning customer breakdown

**Marketing from the database**
- Email / bulk mailing to registered customers
- Push notification campaigns
- Promo codes and loyalty program integration
- Segmentation by: new guests, last order date, order frequency, spend level (Smart/Pro)
