## TL;DR
Own ordering system for delivery and takeaway in the restaurant's brand, **with 0% platform commissions**, connected to Wolt Drive (fixed price) or own couriers. **Available from BASIC** (condition: 70 orders/month; 0% commission from SMART). Main pain: 25–30% Wolt/Bolt/Foodora commissions destroy margins. Main argument: own channel without commissions + direct customer contact.

## What It Is
Own branded ordering system for delivery and takeaway. No commissions and with the option to connect Wolt Drive couriers at a fixed price, or own couriers.

## Who It's For
- Paying high platform commissions (Wolt / Bolt / Foodora)
- Have loyal customers they're unnecessarily paying % for

## Real Example
Sushi bar does orders via Wolt with 30% commission — from a 1,000 CZK (~€40) order, Wolt takes ~300 CZK (~€12). They launch their own Choice ordering system and connect Wolt Drive at a fixed ~80 CZK (~€3) per delivery. On larger orders they save hundreds of CZK each, loyal customers order directly, and contacts and data stay with the restaurant.
