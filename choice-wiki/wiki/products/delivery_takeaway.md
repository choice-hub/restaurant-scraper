# Product: Delivery & Takeaway

## Overview
Choice enables restaurants to accept direct delivery and takeaway orders through their own website and mobile app — with **0% commission**. Customers order directly, cutting out marketplace fees entirely.

## Key Features

**Order Management**
- Order acceptance app (Choice Business) for phone/tablet
- Real-time order notifications
- GPS courier tracking — customers see courier location live
- Order history and customer data captured automatically

**Customer Experience**
- Branded website and mobile app ordering
- 20% conversion rate on order completion
- Dish recommendations to increase average check by up to 20%
- Multiple payment methods: card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash

**Delivery Configuration**
- Custom delivery zones with variable pricing
- Own courier management
- Integration with on-demand courier services: Wolt Drive, Stuart, Glovo on-demand, Uber Direct, Foodora Go

**Business Intelligence**
- Customer database built automatically
- Sales reports: order volume, revenue, popular dishes
- Marketplace vs. direct order performance comparison

## Pricing Model
- **Choice fee:** 0% commission on orders
- **Online payment processing:** 3% (Basic & Standard plans), 0% (Smart & Pro plans — cash/card accepted)
- **Card acquiring fee:** ~1–2% (bank/processor fee, not Choice)

## Business Impact
| Metric | Improvement |
|--------|------------|
| Profit increase vs marketplaces | Up to 30% |
| Customer loyalty | Up to 30% improvement |
| Setup time | 1–5 days |

## Plans
Available on all plans. On-demand courier integrations available on all plans.
