# Product: Delivery & Takeaway

**URL:** https://choiceqr.com/products-en/delivery/

## Overview
Launch your own delivery and takeout system with **0% commission on orders**. 90% of customers subscribe to Choice within the first week of implementing the delivery service. Save up to 30% on commissions compared to third-party marketplaces.

## Key Features

**Your Own Branded Website & App**
- Fully branded website in your brand colors with logo and individual settings
- 20% conversion rate on order completion
- Dish recommendations increase average check by up to 20%
- 90% of users access via mobile — fully responsive

**Order Management**
- Choice Business app (phone/tablet) for real-time order management
- GPS courier tracking — customers see courier location live
- Auto-captures customer database with every order
- Real-time notifications for new orders

**Delivery Configuration**
- Define any delivery zones in the city with variable pricing per zone
- Set different delivery times and packaging fees per zone
- Minimum order amounts, promo codes, discounts
- Own courier management + app for couriers

**On-Demand Courier Integrations**
- Wolt Drive, Stuart, Glovo on-demand, Uber Direct, Foodora Go
- Use when own couriers are unavailable or for overflow

**Payment Methods**
- Bank cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, cash
- Online payment fee: 3% (Basic/Standard plans), 0% (Smart/Pro plans)
