## TL;DR
QR menu, table orders, and table payments: guest scans code, orders and/or pays without waiting — all connected to POS. **QR payments available from BASIC.** Main pain: overwhelmed service during peak, waiting, walkouts. Main argument: faster service, higher revenue, more tips, fewer walkouts.

## What It Is
Three modes — which are enabled depends on the restaurant setup:

1. **QR Menu** — display menu only (no ordering)
2. **QR Orders** — guest orders from their phone, order appears in POS
3. **QR Payments** — guest pays their bill by scanning QR at the table

Modes can be combined. Everything connects to the POS; staff have a real-time overview.

## Who It's For
- Bistros, cafés, and venues with fast table turnover
- Terraces or venues with multiple floors
- Busy restaurants during lunch and dinner
- City centres and tourist locations (auto-translated menu)
- NOT recommended for small venues or fine dining where classic service is preferred

## Key Features
**QR Menu**
- Instant access — scan → full menu with photos and videos, no app, no registration
- 99-language automatic translation — Chinese, Italian, German tourists all self-served
- Rich media: photos, videos per dish
- Real-time updates — price changes, item availability, specials; no reprinting
- Promotional tools: Happy Hours, Business Lunch, banners
- Wishlist — guests save dishes and show selection to the waiter
- Interactive business card: hours, Wi-Fi password, address, social links

**QR Orders**
- Per-table QR codes — each table has a unique code
- Orders arrive instantly on staff tablet and/or POS
- Staff can accept/reject orders from the app
- Upsell engine: recommendations and wishlist increase average check
- Customer data collected automatically into CRM with every order

**QR Payments**
- Guest pays whenever it's convenient — no waiting for the check
- Saves guest 20–30% of their time; increases table turnover by 30%
- **No app, no registration** — works in any mobile browser, with all banks
- **Apple Pay, Google Pay** supported
- Bill splitting — guests split equally or assign specific items per person
- Tips — digital tip prompts increase tips by 20% (guests tip even without spare change)
- Reduces walkouts — guest can pay anytime without waiting
