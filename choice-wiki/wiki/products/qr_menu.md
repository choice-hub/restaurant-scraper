# Product: QR Menu / Contactless Menu

## Overview
A digital menu customers access by scanning a QR code — no app download required. Works in any mobile browser. Displays photos, videos, dish descriptions, allergens, and promotions.

## Key Features

- **Instant access** — Scan QR → view menu immediately, no registration
- **99-language auto-translation** — Works for international guests automatically
- **Rich media** — Photos, videos per dish; increases appetite and average check
- **Real-time updates** — Change prices, 86 items, add specials instantly
- **Promotional tools** — Happy hours, business lunch menus, banners
- **Wishlist** — Guests save dishes and show their selection to the waiter
- **Feedback collection** — Catches unhappy guests before they post a bad review
- **Interactive business card** — Hours, Wi-Fi password, address, social links, reviews
- **Analytics** — Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel integration
- **Brand customization** — Matches restaurant's logo, colors, fonts

## How It Works
1. Upload menu in admin panel
2. Print QR code (Choice provides printable materials)
3. Customer scans → views menu on their phone
4. Updates sync instantly — no reprint needed

## Business Impact
| Metric | Improvement |
|--------|------------|
| Cost reduction (vs paper menus) | ~20% |
| Average check increase | ~15% |
| Negative review reduction | ~30% |
