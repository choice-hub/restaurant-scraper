# Product: QR Menu / Digital Menu

**URL:** https://choiceqr.com/products-en/contactless-menu/

## Overview
A modern QR-based digital menu designed to boost sales and improve staff efficiency. Accessible via QR code or link — **no app required, no registration**. 30% conversion rate.

## Key Features

**Access & Usability**
- Scan QR code or open link → instant menu access, no download, no registration
- Works on any smartphone in any mobile browser
- 99-language automatic translation — serves international guests instantly

**Rich Menu Display**
- Attractive photos and videos per dish — increases appetite and average check
- Detailed descriptions, allergen information, portion sizes
- Real-time updates — change prices, 86 items, add specials instantly; no reprinting

**More Than a Menu — Digital Business Card**
- Opening hours, contact info, Wi-Fi password
- Address with map, other locations
- Social media links
- Leave a review directly from the menu

**Promotions & Upsell**
- Happy Hours — automatic time-based price changes
- Business Lunch menus
- Promotional banners
- Wishlist — guests save dishes and show selection to waiter

**Feedback**
- In-menu feedback collection catches unhappy guests before they post publicly
- Positive reviews redirected to Google Maps

**Analytics**
- Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel integration
- Track popular dishes, peak hours, customer behavior
- Best-seller analytics

**Brand Customization**
- Matches restaurant's logo, colors, fonts

## How It Works
1. Upload menu in admin panel (photos, prices, descriptions)
2. Print QR code (Choice provides printable materials)
3. Customer scans → views menu on their phone
4. Updates sync instantly — no reprint needed
