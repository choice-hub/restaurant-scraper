## TL;DR
Consolidate all delivery platforms (Wolt, Bolt, Foodora, Bistro, soon Uber Eats CZ) into **one app and one tablet**, with optional POS connection. **PRO plan** (or as a module). Main pain: chaos from multiple tablets and admins + manual entry. Main argument: one tablet, one menu, overview of all platforms.

## What It Is
Consolidation of all delivery marketplace platforms into one app and one tablet. Orders from Wolt, Bolt, Foodora, Bistro — and soon Uber Eats on the CZ market — all in one place. Optional POS connection.

## Who It's For
- Have 2+ delivery platforms (and therefore 2+ tablets)
- Chaos in order reception, manual entry into POS
- Updating menus in several admin panels
- Don't know which platform performs best

## Key Features
**Unified Order Management**
- All marketplace orders in one application on one tablet
- Orders labeled with each platform's logo and colors
- Direct orders (own delivery site) and marketplace orders in the same interface
- Single notification center — no missed orders

**Menu Management**
- Add and modify items in Choice — automatically synced to all connected marketplaces
- Edit prices, names, availability per platform independently
- One admin for all marketplace menus

**POS Integration**
- Orders automatically transferred to POS — no manual entry
- Integrates with Dotykačka, Storyous (primary), and others
- Eliminates errors from manual input

**Analytics**
- Track volume, average order value, popular dishes per platform
- Compare platform performance vs. direct orders
- Revenue breakdown per channel

Restaurant has three tablets (Wolt, Bolt, Foodora) on the counter, manually enters orders into the POS, and chaos erupts during peak hours.
