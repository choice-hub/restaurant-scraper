## TL;DR
Marketing tools (CRM, promo codes, loyalty/cashback, gifts, mailing) help the restaurant **reclaim customers from platforms into their own database**. **SMART / PRO plans.** Main pain: all data and contacts stay with Wolt/Bolt. Main argument: own database + repeat orders directly.

## What It Is
Tools for gaining control over customers instead of dependence on platforms (Wolt, Bolt). Collect data, motivate repeat orders, build the restaurant's own database and brand.

**Included:**
- **Customer database (CRM)** — overview of orders, contacts, and favourite items
- **Promo codes and discounts**
- **Loyalty program** — cashback (e.g. 10%), points system
- **Gifts** on order (e.g. free item over 500 CZK / €20)
- **Bulk mailing** to registered customers
- **SMS marketing** — retention, promotions (Smart/Pro)

Data is automatically collected from all channels — **QR, delivery, takeaway, and reservations** — into one database.

## Who It's For
- High order volume through platforms with high commissions

**SMS Marketing** (Smart/Pro)
- Targeted SMS campaigns with promotions

## Real Example (Storytelling)
A sushi restaurant sends orders through Wolt paying 30% commission. They partner with **Studio TD** who makes branded packaging boxes. Each box has a **QR code** leading to their Choice e-shop with: *"Use code SAVE10 on your first order and get 10% cashback."*

Next time the customer scans the QR instead of opening Wolt → gets a discount → earns cashback → orders directly. The restaurant gets the contact (own CRM), order history, and ability to reach the customer via mailing. This is how a restaurant **gradually reclaims customers from Wolt into its own database**.
