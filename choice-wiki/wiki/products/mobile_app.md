## What It Is
A fully branded mobile app for iOS and Android where customers have the restaurant in their pocket. From one app they can:
- Order **delivery and takeaway**
- Make **reservations**
- Browse the **menu** or use the **QR scanner**
- Receive **push notifications** with news and promotions

Connected to all Choice features — same login, loyalty points, and order history as on the website.

## Who It's For
- **Chains and franchises** — branded app for all branches
- **Venues with regular customers** where communicating news and promotions makes sense
- **Cafés, fast food, and bistros** with high online order volume
- Makes most sense for stronger brands; for single venues, start with web, QR, and delivery first

## Key Features
- **Full branding** — your logo, colors, fonts; indistinguishable from a custom-built app
- **All ordering channels** — delivery, takeaway, table reservations, QR menu
- **Push notifications** — targeted promotions, order status, marketing campaigns (free channel)
- **Loyalty program** — points, cashback, promo codes built in
- **Payment methods** — card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Multi-location** — one app for all branches with location selector
- **POS integration** — orders sync to kitchen systems
- **Reviews** — customers leave feedback within the app

- **All data synced** — no duplication with web/QR/delivery, same backend

## Real Example
A chain of venues with a regular clientele depends on Wolt and Instagram — paying commissions and fighting algorithms. They launch their own branded app: customers order and make reservations directly from the app, the venue sends push notifications as a **free marketing channel**, loyalty points and history sync across all Choice tools. The customer stays theirs, not Wolt's.
