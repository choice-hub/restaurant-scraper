# Product: Custom Mobile App (iOS & Android)

## Overview
A fully branded, custom mobile app for your restaurant published on the App Store and Google Play under your brand name. Built and deployed by Choice — no separate developer needed.

## Key Features

- **Full branding** — Your logo, colors, fonts; indistinguishable from a custom-built app
- **All ordering channels** — Delivery, takeaway, table reservations, QR menu
- **Push notifications** — Targeted promotions, order status, marketing campaigns
- **Loyalty program** — Points, cashback, promo codes built in
- **Payment methods** — Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Multi-location** — One app for all branches with location selector
- **POS integration** — Orders sync to kitchen systems
- **Reviews** — Customers leave feedback within the app
- **Analytics** — Full order and customer behavior tracking
