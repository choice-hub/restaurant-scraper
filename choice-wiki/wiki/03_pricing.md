# Choice Pricing

## Pricing Structure

Choice uses a **fixed monthly subscription** with no commission on direct orders. Two account types: **Single Location** and **Multi-location (Chain)**.

### Billing Discounts
| Billing Period | Discount |
|---------------|---------|
| Monthly | Standard price |
| 12 months (annual) | −10% |
| 24 months | −20% |

> **Ukraine exception:** billing discounts are 6 months −10% / 12 months −20%.

## Plan Feature Breakdown

### Basic — Free
> For small restaurants getting started online. Requires minimum 70 orders/month.

**Online payment fee:** 3% on delivery & pickup orders (online payments only — cash not supported)

**Included features:**
- Website + own domain
- Online ordering — delivery + takeaway
- Integrations with courier services: Wolt Drive, Glovo on-demand, Uber Direct, Foodora Go, and others
- QR Menu
- QR table ordering
- QR Payment at the table
- Waiter tips
- Restaurant tips
- Advanced Customer Database and CRM
- Upsell features
- Promo codes
- Email marketing + automated retention notifications
- SEO optimization
- Marketing analytics (Google Tag, Facebook Pixel, Google integration)
- Feedback collection via Google Maps — automatically sends positive reviews to Google
- Dashboard with all channels and clients overview
- Best seller analytics
- AI Photo editor — **20 generations/month**
- Support: Standard level

---

### Standard — from €32/month *(see regional prices below)*
> For busy places ready to grow sales.

**Online payment fee:** 3% on delivery & pickup orders (online payments only)

**Everything in Basic, plus:**
- Table reservations — Google integrations, calendar, management tools *(included, no add-on fee)*
- AI Photo editor — **40 generations/month** *(vs 20 in Basic)*
- Support: Standard level

---

### Smart — from €68/month *(see regional prices below)* ⭐ POPULAR
> For restaurants focused on customer retention.

**Online payment fee:** 0% *(both online payments and cash accepted)*

**Everything in Standard, plus:**
- Collection Point — Wolt, Glovo, Bolt, Foodora, Uber Eats, Just Eat Takeaway orders on 1 tablet
- Marketplace Menu Management — manage all marketplace menus from one place
- Cashback — points system across all order types
- Presents (Gifts)
- SMS Marketing + automated retention notifications
- AI Photo editor — **60 generations/month**
- Support: Priority level
- 1+1 bonus system / Happy hours *(coming soon)*
- App for own couriers *(coming soon)*
- Courier Management *(coming soon)*

---

### Pro — from €100/month *(see regional prices below)*
> For multi-location restaurants and advanced operations.

**Online payment fee:** 0% *(both online payments and cash accepted)*

**Everything in Smart, plus:**
- Own restaurant app for clients — your branded app on App Store and Google Play
- POS Integration — Storyous, Dotykačka, Serviop, R-Keeper, etc.
- Cross-restaurant loyalty program — for chains or multi-brand locations
- Single admin panel for all locations
- Customer base segmentation *(coming soon)* — filter by new guests, last order date, order count, and other criteria
- AI Photo editor — **200 generations/month + expansion**
- Support: Dedicated key account manager (CSM)
- AI Assistants *(coming soon)*:
  - AI Text edit and translations
  - Analytics assistant — ask anything about your restaurant performance
  - Review reply assistant — generate replies to customer feedback
  - Marketing content generator — create posts, campaigns, and promotions

## Payment Processing Fees

Choice uses the **IC++ (Interchange++)** model — fully transparent, industry-standard.

### Delivery & Takeaway orders
- **1.3% + 2.50 CZK** (CZ)
- **1.3% + €0.10** (EUR markets)

### QR Payments / Table Orders
- **0.99% + 2.50 CZK** (CZ)
- **0.99% + €0.10** (EUR markets)

> Special pricing possible for high-volume clients or competitive situations — requires approval.

### Service Fee options (passed to end customer or absorbed)
| Option | Rate |
|--------|------|
| Fixed fee | 2.50 CZK / €0.10 |
| % passed to customer | 1–1.5% (min 2.50 CZK / €0.10, max 26 CZK / €1.00) |

### IC++ breakdown (informational)
| Card type | Typical range |
|-----------|--------------|
| Standard debit (EU) | 0.25–0.35% |
| Credit (EU) | 0.35–0.45% |
| Premium / corporate / foreign | 0.7–2% |

### Other fees (Adyen)
| Fee | Rate |
|-----|------|
| Payout of funds | €0.33/transfer |
| Refund | €0.10 (free within 24 hours) |
| Chargeback | €18.18 |
| Cross-border payout | €10.76 |
| American Express | 3.30–3.45% |
| 3DS authentication | €0.05/request |

> Funds reach the restaurant's bank account within **48 hours** of the order.
