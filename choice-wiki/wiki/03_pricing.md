# Choice Pricing

## Pricing Structure

Choice uses a **fixed monthly subscription** model with no commission on direct orders. Two account types exist: **Single Location** and **Multi-location (Chain)**.

### Billing Discounts
| Billing Period | Discount |
|---------------|---------|
| Monthly | Standard price |
| 12 months (annual) | –10% |
| 24 months | –20% |

---

## Plans — Single Location

### Basic — Free
**Best for:** Small restaurants getting started online. Requires minimum 70 orders/month.

**Online payment fee:**
- Delivery & Pickup orders: **3%**
- Condition: Online payments only (no cash orders on Basic)

**Included features:**
- Website + own domain
- Online ordering (delivery + takeaway)
- QR Menu
- QR orders to table
- QR payment at the table
- Waiter tips & restaurant tips
- Integrations with courier services (Wolt Drive, Glovo on-demand, Uber Direct, Foodora Go)
- Advanced Customer Database and CRM
- Upsell features
- Promo codes
- Email marketing
- Automated retention notifications
- SEO optimization
- Marketing analytics
- Google Tag / Facebook Pixel integration
- Feedback collection + Google Maps review forwarding
- Dashboard with all channels overview
- Best-seller analytics
- AI Photo editor (20 generations/month)
- Standard support

**Add-ons available (not included):**
- Table reservations: **€40/mo**
- Collection Point (marketplace aggregator): **€40/mo**
- App for own couriers (coming soon): **€20/mo**
- Own restaurant apps (iOS + Android): **€40/mo**

---

### Standard — €32/month
**Best for:** Busy places ready to grow sales.

**Online payment fee:**
- Delivery & Pickup orders: **3%**
- Condition: Online payments only

**Includes everything in Basic, plus:**
- Table reservations (included, no add-on fee)
- Google integrations, calendar, management tools
- AI Photo editor (40 generations/month)
- Standard support

---

### Smart — €68/month ⭐ POPULAR
**Best for:** Restaurants focused on retention.

**Online payment fee:**
- Delivery & Pickup orders: **0%**
- Condition: Online payments AND cash accepted

**Includes everything in Standard, plus:**
- Collection Point (marketplace aggregator — Wolt, Glovo, Bolt, Foodora, Uber, Just Eat on 1 tablet)
- Marketplace Menu Management (manage all marketplace menus from one place)
- Cashback (points system across all order types)
- Presents / Gifts
- SMS Marketing
- Automated retention notifications
- AI Photo editor (60 generations/month)
- Priority support
- 1+1 bonus system / Happy hours *(coming soon)*
- App for own couriers *(coming soon)*
- Courier Management *(coming soon)*

---

### Pro — €100/month
**Best for:** Multi-location restaurants and advanced operations.

**Online payment fee:**
- Delivery & Pickup orders: **0%**
- Condition: Online payments AND cash accepted

**Includes everything in Smart, plus:**
- Own restaurant apps for clients (iOS + Android — your branded app on App Store + Google Play)
- POS Integration (Storyous, Dotykačka, Serviop, R-Keeper, etc.)
- Cross-restaurant loyalty program
- Chains / multi-brand location support
- Single admin panel for all locations
- Customer base segmentation *(coming soon)*
- AI Photo editor (200 generations/month + expansion)
- Dedicated Key Account Manager (CSM)
- AI Assistants *(coming soon)*:
  - AI Text edit and translations
  - Generate replies to customer feedback
  - Analytics assistant ("ask anything about your restaurant performance")
  - Review reply assistant
  - Marketing content generator

## Payment Processing Fees

Choice uses the **Interchange++** model for card payments:

| Fee Component | Rate |
|--------------|------|
| Interchange (EU debit) | from 0.2% |
| Interchange (EU credit) | up to 0.3% |
| Card scheme fees | ~0.16%–1.12% + €0.13–€1.00/transaction |
| Choice processing fee | 1.3% + €0.10/transaction |
| Payout of funds | €0.33/transfer |
| Refund | €0.10 (free if within 24 hours) |
| Chargeback | €18.18 |
| Cross-border payout | €10.76 |
| American Express | 3.30–3.45% |
| 3DS authentication | €0.05/request |
