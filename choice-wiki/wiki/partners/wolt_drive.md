# Partner: Wolt Drive

**Type:** Courier / LaaS (Logistics as a Service)
**Available from:** BASIC plan (min. 70 orders/month); SMART+ = no 3% delivery commission
**Notion:** https://app.notion.com/p/311178865343802882f6f0ef53b500f1

## What It Is
Wolt Drive delivers orders from point A to point B for a fixed fee based on straight-line distance from the restaurant address. Restaurants don't need their own couriers — Wolt handles dispatch.

The cost can be split between the restaurant and the customer. Average customer-paid delivery price via Choice: **82 CZK (CZ) / €2.26 (SK)**.

## Extended Radius

For longer distances — priced from the 1st km with a surcharge per each started 500 m.

### Czech Republic (excl. VAT)
- 1st km: **99 CZK**
- +7 CZK per started 500 m above 1 km
- Max charge: **225 CZK** (up to 10 km)

**Examples:** 1.2 km → 106 CZK | 1.7 km → 113 CZK | 2.1 km → 120 CZK

### Slovakia (excl. VAT)
- 1st km: **€3.50**
- +€0.25 per started 500 m above 1 km
- Max charge: **€10.25** (up to 15 km)

**Examples:** 1.2 km → €3.75 | 1.7 km → €4.00 | 2.1 km → €4.25

## Sales Notes
- Available from BASIC — good for smaller restaurants starting their own delivery
- On SMART+: no additional 3% delivery commission from Choice
- Restaurant sets their own delivery fee charged to the customer (e.g. 40 CZK) → net cost can be as low as ~50 CZK/delivery
- Two pricing models (Flat Fee vs. Zones) — Zones better for restaurants with short avg. delivery distance
