## About BiteBerry
Slovak tech startup focused on **digitising restaurant delivery and automating orders via AI**. ~15 person team. Builds a Delivery Management System (DMS) for own-courier operations and an AI Voice Ordering system for phone orders.

Integrates with: Dotykačka, Syrve, 3S POS, Wolt, Delivery Hero, Uber Eats, Foodora, Deliverect, and others.

## Products

### AI Voice Ordering
- Automatic phone order taking via AI voice assistant — no new hardware needed
- Customer calls the restaurant's normal number → AI processes order → connects to POS → confirms to customer
- Customisable voice, languages, kitchen system integration
- **Price: 5% of order value, min. €1/order**

### Delivery Management System (DMS)
Full system for managing own-courier delivery:
- Centralised dispatch (manual or automatic)
- Real-time GPS courier tracking
- Driver App (multi-pickup, multi-drop, delivery confirmation)
- Kitchen App (order prioritisation, label printing)
- Customer order tracker (status, ETA, courier chat)
- Multi-branch and dark kitchen support
- **Price: €75/month/venue** or **€0.10/order** (for delivery companies)

## Choice Integration
Orders created in Choice (QR, website, Collection Point) → automatically pushed to BiteBerry DMS → dispatched to couriers → delivery tracked → status updated back in Choice.

Integration is functional and stable. For complex setups (e.g. combined orders) some manual configuration may be needed.

**Best fit:** restaurants with their own courier fleet that want complete delivery control without external systems.
