# Partner: Foodora GO

**Type:** Courier / LaaS (Logistics as a Service)
**Availability:** Czech Republic only
**Status:** Active (CZ); Coming soon elsewhere
**Notion:** https://app.notion.com/p/37917886534380ef93c6f85427adf2f4

## What It Is
Foodora GO is a delivery partner (similar model to Wolt Drive) that handles order dispatch from Choice. Pricing depends on the **city price group** and **straight-line distance** from the restaurant.

Cost can be split between restaurant and customer. Average customer-paid delivery via Choice: **82 CZK**.

All prices excl. VAT.
