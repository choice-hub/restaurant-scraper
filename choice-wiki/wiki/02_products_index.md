## Key Integrations

- **Delivery Marketplaces:** Wolt, Bolt, Glovo, Uber Eats, Foodora, Just Eat Takeaway
- **On-demand Couriers:** Wolt Drive, Stuart, Glovo on-demand, Uber Direct, Foodora Go
- **POS Systems:** Poster, Syrve (iiko), Serviop, Profit, Compucash, R-Keeper, Dotykačka, Storyous
- **Payments:** Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay (via Adyen, Fondy, LiqPay)
- **Marketing:** Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Google Tag Manager
- **Reviews:** Google Maps, TripAdvisor
