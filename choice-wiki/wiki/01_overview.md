# Choice — Company & Platform Overview

## What is Choice?

Choice (choiceqr.com) is an **all-in-one digital platform for restaurants, bars, cafés, and hotels**. It enables food & hospitality businesses to manage online ordering, QR menus, table ordering, payments, reservations, delivery, and marketing — all from a single admin panel at a fixed monthly price with **zero commission on direct orders**.

**Tagline:** "The ultimate all-in-one solution for restaurants"

## What Problems Choice Solves

1. **High marketplace commissions** — Wolt, Glovo, Bolt etc. charge 25–35% per order. Choice lets restaurants take direct orders at 0% commission.
2. **Fragmented operations** — Managing orders from multiple sources (website, marketplaces, tables) across different devices. Choice unifies everything.
3. **Expensive tech stack** — Building a website, app, POS integration, loyalty program separately costs tens of thousands. Choice bundles it all for €32–100/mo.
4. **No customer data** — Marketplace orders don't give restaurants their customers' data. Choice builds a owned customer database.
