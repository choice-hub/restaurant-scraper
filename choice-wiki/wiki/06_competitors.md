## Quick Competitive Arguments

**vs Qerko** (top priority)
- Choice payment fee is lower in 99% of cases — calculate from actual monthly statement
- Customer data fully owned by restaurant (name, contacts, history) — Qerko only gives first name + last payment time
- Qerko app shows competitors' promotions to your guests
- Choice = full platform (orders, reservations, website, loyalty); Qerko = QR payments only

**vs DISH (Makro/Metro)**
- DISH is cheap but outdated and fragmented; no POS/Apple Pay/Wolt Drive in CZ

**vs Objednáme.cz**
- No commission model similar, but weak marketing, no QR/table system, no reservations; Wolt Drive integration unreliable

**vs Speedlo**
- 10% portal commission + paid modules = high TCO; no table QR or reservations

**vs Adaptee**
- Strong in delivery (incl. Wolt Drive), weak dine-in; Choice + Wolt Drive without % per meal

**vs Restia**
- Orders + Collection Point only (R-Keeper); note: Restia is the tech behind Qerko Qpoint

**vs BookioPro**
- Good reservations (no commission), but nothing else; Choice = full platform

**vs Apetee**
- Reservation + portal/catalogue; guest goes through Apetee brand, not yours

**vs Rej Dílky**
- Portal + PR only; no delivery/CP/deposits; reminders sent in their name

**vs Tableo**
- International reservation tool (free up to 100 guests), weak CZ support; reservations only

**vs Restaumatic**
- Polish company, strong on SK; charges % of orders, support only Mon–Fri until 14:00, payment gateway messages in Polish, reservations by email only

## Choice's Unique Position
Choice is the **only platform** in the CZ/SK market that combines in a single subscription:
- Own restaurant website (SEO, mobile, custom domain)
- Online ordering — delivery + takeaway (0% commission on Smart/Pro)
- QR table orders + QR payments
- Table reservation system
- Collection Point (all marketplaces on 1 tablet)
- CRM + loyalty program
- Marketing tools (SMS, email, push)
- POS integration
- Mobile app (Pro)
