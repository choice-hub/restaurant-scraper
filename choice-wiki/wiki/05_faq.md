# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Q: What is Choice?**
Choice is an all-in-one digital platform for restaurants, bars, cafés, and hotels. It provides online ordering, QR menus, table ordering, payments, reservations, delivery integrations, CRM, and loyalty — all from a single admin panel at a fixed monthly price.

**Q: How is Choice different from Wolt/Glovo/Uber Eats?**
Marketplaces charge 25–35% commission per order. Choice lets restaurants take direct orders through their own website or app at 0% commission. Choice also integrates WITH those marketplaces via Collection Point so you can manage all orders in one place.

**Q: How quickly can I get started?**
The website and QR menu are ready within 48 hours of registration. Full setup including POS integration takes 1–7 days.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
Yes, 14 days free, no credit card required.

**Q: Does Choice work in my country?**
Choice operates in: Czech Republic, Slovakia, Poland, Ukraine, Hungary, Estonia, Lithuania, Latvia, Romania, Germany, Ireland, and globally. Check choiceqr.com for your region.

## Pricing & Plans

**Q: What plans does Choice offer?**
Four plans: Basic (Free), Standard (€32/mo), Smart (€68/mo), Pro (€100/mo). See [Pricing](03_pricing.md) for full details.

**Q: Is the Basic plan really free?**
Yes, but it requires a minimum of 70 orders/month and charges a 3% fee on online payments. For restaurants with low volume, this may not be the right fit.

**Q: What's the difference between Smart and Pro?**
Smart adds loyalty, gifts, SMS marketing, and marketplace integration. Pro adds custom mobile apps, advanced POS integration, multi-location management, AI tools, and a dedicated account manager.

**Q: Can I change plans later?**
Yes, plans can be changed at any time through the admin panel.

**Q: Are there commissions on orders?**
- Basic & Standard: 3% fee on online payments only
- Smart & Pro: 0% — both online and cash orders

## Products

**Q: Do customers need to download an app to use QR menus or ordering?**
No. All QR menu and ordering features work in any mobile browser — no app download required.

**Q: Can I manage orders from Wolt, Glovo, and my own website in one place?**
Yes — that's what Collection Point does. Available on Smart & Pro plans, or as a €40/mo add-on on Basic/Standard.

**Q: Which POS systems does Choice integrate with?**
Poster, Syrve (iiko), Serviop, Profit, Compucash, R-Keeper, Dotykačka, Storyous, and others.

**Q: Can guests split the bill with QR payment?**
Yes. The QR Payment feature supports bill splitting per item and per person, with individual tipping.

**Q: Does the loyalty program work across multiple locations?**
Yes, on the Pro plan. Cross-restaurant loyalty allows guests to earn and spend points across all your locations and brands.

**Q: Can I have my own mobile app on the App Store?**
Yes — Choice builds and publishes a fully branded iOS + Android app for €500 one-time setup fee (Pro plan required).

## Technical

**Q: What devices does Choice work on?**
The admin panel works on any web browser. The Choice Business app (for staff) is on iOS and Android. Customer-facing features work on any smartphone browser.

**Q: How does menu translation work?**
Choice automatically translates your menu into 99 languages using AI translation. Customers see the menu in their device's language.

**Q: How do I connect my Wolt account to Choice?**
Go to Admin Panel → Integrations → Marketplace → Wolt. Enter your Wolt Store ID. Your Choice menu will sync to Wolt automatically.

**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
Bank cards (Visa, Mastercard), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash (Smart & Pro plans).
